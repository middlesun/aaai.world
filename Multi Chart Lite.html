Help & Examples

Basic Usage

Enter a symbol, time interval, and indicators separated by spaces.

Example: AAPL 1D RSI MACD

Symbol Formats

  • AAPL - Apple Inc.
  • NASDAQ:AAPL - Exchange prefix
  • BTCUSD - Cryptocurrencies

Time Intervals

1 5 15 60 D W M

Available Indicators

RSI MACD BB EMA SMA VWAP Stoch ADX ATR
